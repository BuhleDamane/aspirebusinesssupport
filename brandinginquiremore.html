The National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS) is a South African government-funded program that provides financial assistance to eligible students for tuition, accommodation, and other study-related costs. It is designed to support students from low-income households who wish to pursue higher education at public universities and TVET colleges. We assist with NSFAS applications. Payment is required upfront, and applications are processed within 72 hours.

We assist in applying to universities and colleges across South Africa. Our service covers applications to all 26 public universities and various TVET colleges. Important: This service is for Tertiary admission applications only. It does not include residence applications, which must be completed separately. Payment is required upfront, and the application process begins only upon receipt of proof of payment. Please note that our service fee does not cover Tertiary application fees, which must be paid separately by the student. Applications will be processed and completed within 72 hours of receiving payment confirmation.

We assist with Tertiary residence applications across South Africa.

Difference Between Tertiary Admission and Residence Application:

- A university admission application is for securing a spot in a degree, diploma, or certificate program at a university or college.

- A residence application is for securing on-campus or university-managed accommodation for students. This is a separate process from academic admission.

Why Is a Residence Application Important?

- On-campus housing provides safe, convenient, and affordable accommodation.

- Some residences offer meals, study spaces, and social facilities that enhance student life.

- Spaces are limited, so early application is crucial.

Application Process:

- Payment is upfront, and the process begins only after we receive proof of payment.

- Our service fee does not cover university residence fees, which must be paid separately by the student.

- Residence applications will be processed and completed within 72 hours of payment confirmation.

We apply for bursaries on your behalf. Our team searches for bursaries that best match your eligibility, but you are also welcome to recommend any specific bursaries you would like to apply for. As part of our service, we provide guidance on what to include in a bursary application, assist with writing cover letters, and proofread documents to ensure they meet the necessary standards. Once everything is prepared, we submit the application on your behalf and provide confirmation. If bursary applications are currently closed, we offer guidance on when they will open and how to prepare in advance.

Since we live in a technology-driven world, Aspire Business Support has taken it upon itself not only to assist students with their applications but also to handle their registration process. Upon admission to a tertiary institution, we will research the required modules, complete the registration process on behalf of the student, and resolve any registration-related issues that can be handled online. Our aim is to ensure a seamless transition into tertiary education, allowing students to focus on their studies without unnecessary delays or stress.
