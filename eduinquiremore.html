Our Education Services are tailored to help students with university applications, bursary and NSFAS applications, as well as tutoring in various fields. We aim to simplify the application process and make education more accessible.

Individual Services

University Application (R50.00): A straightforward application service where we assist students in applying to their chosen universities.

NSFAS Application (R20.00): Help with applying for the National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS). A simple, affordable service that includes guidance through the process.

Bursary Application (R150.00): Assistance with bursary applications, including review of application documents. Please note, while we do not write motivation letters for students, we offer guidance and review to ensure that applications are as strong as possible.

Student Accommodation Application (R50.00): We help students with accommodation applications to secure housing near their university. This service will help students find a comfortable living space while studying.

Tertiary Registration (R300.00): This service is only paid upon successful admission to a tertiary institution. It helps students save time and money by handling the registration process, ensuring a seamless transition to campus without the need for physical visits.
